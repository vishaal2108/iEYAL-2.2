## Features

*(What it is → Why it matters → Business value)*

**Drag-and-Drop Flow Builder**

* **What it is:** A visual builder for creating automated conversation flows — no code required.
* **Why it matters:** Most WhatsApp automation tools require a developer. Ownchat's flow builder is designed for business owners to build and edit flows themselves.
* **Business value / Customer outcome:** Faster response times and consistent answers, without hiring technical staff.

**Team Inbox**

* **What it is:** A shared inbox where every team member sees, claims, and responds to conversations in one place.
* **Why it matters:** Eliminates the chaos of multiple people replying from one phone, or conversations getting lost between shifts.
* **Business value / Customer outcome:** Faster resolution, full conversation history, and accountability per team member.

**Auto Replies**

* **What it is:** Instant automated responses triggered by keywords, time of day, or customer actions.
* **Why it matters:** Customers expect a response within minutes — auto replies buy your team time without leaving anyone waiting.
* **Business value / Customer outcome:** Higher customer satisfaction and lower response-time complaints.

**Broadcasts**

* **What it is:** Bulk, compliant WhatsApp messaging to segmented customer lists — offers, updates, reminders.
* **Why it matters:** Email open rates are falling; WhatsApp messages get read within minutes.
* **Business value / Customer outcome:** Higher campaign engagement and more repeat purchases with less marketing spend.
* **Icon suggestion:** One distinct icon per feature (flow/branch icon, inbox icon, lightning-reply icon, megaphone icon)
* **UI component suggestion:** 2x2 feature card grid with screenshot thumbnail per card
